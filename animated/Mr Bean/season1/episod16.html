Magpie

Season 1,Episode 16

About

In this episode, Mr. Bean brings an injured magpie to his flat, to which he realises that it likes to collect lovely and shiny things taken from neighbours' houses. When Mrs. Wicket and the policeman see this, Bean is wrongly arrested for grand thievery. When the policeman returns from his car after having a parking fine, he sees the magpie holding his keys, realizing that the magpie was responsible for theft. Bean is finally released from custody, but when he returns to his flat, he finds the Queen's crown stolen by the magpie, which shocks him.
