Cat-Sitting

Season 1,Episode 17

About

In this episode, When Mrs. Wicket is hospitalized after accidentally breaking her arm, Mr. Bean must look after Scrapper while she is away. Unfortunately, this is difficult since they hate each other. When everything becomes a mess, Bean gets assaulted by Mrs. Wicket when she returns from the hospital and as a result, he is also sent to the hospital, where on the way there via ambulance, Scrapper has stowed away.
