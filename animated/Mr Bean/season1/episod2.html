Missing Teddy

Season 1,Episode 2

About

In this episode, Mr. Bean's beloved teddy bear goes missing, and he embarks on a frantic search to find it. He retraces his steps from the previous day, which included a trip to the park and a visit to a toy store. Along the way, he encounters various obstacles, including a troublesome squirrel and a group of mischievous children who steal his clothes.
