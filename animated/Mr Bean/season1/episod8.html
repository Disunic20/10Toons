The Mole

Season 1,Episode 8

About

In this episode, Mrs. Wicket's croquet game is interrupted by an offending mole. Mr. Bean tries to get rid of it, but to no avail until he finally manages to capture it. Mrs. Wicket then orders Bean to kill it with a croquet hammer, but Bean, having mercy not to kill it, puts it into a jar and releases it into the wild.
