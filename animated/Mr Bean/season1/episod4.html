Bean's Bounty

Season 1,Episode 4

About

In this episode, Mr. Bean builds his own metal detector and goes to hunt for treasure, but fails. When he finally manages to find treasure and brings it to his flat, the burglar duo follow him and tie him up but Mrs. Wicket confronts them and the police arrive, arresting the duo once again. However, Mrs. Wicket and Scrapper also get arrested as Mrs. Wicket's mud mask looks like a thief's mask.
