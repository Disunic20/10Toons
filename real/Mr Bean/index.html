Mr. Bean

Mr. Bean is a British sitcom created by Rowan Atkinson and Richard Curtis, produced by Tiger Aspect and starring Atkinson as the title character. The sitcom consists of 15 episodes that were co-written by Atkinson alongside Curtis and Robin Driscoll; for the pilot, it was co-written by Ben Elton. The series was originally broadcast on ITV, beginning with the pilot on 1 January 1990 and ending with "The Best Bits of Mr. Bean" on 15 December 1995.

Total 1 Season and 15 Episodes

1.Mr. Bean

Mr Bean rushes to his exam only to find out he's studied the wrong subject.

2.The Return of Mr. Bean

Mr Bean wants to give a busker some money until he realises he has no change, while afterwards he goes shopping but gets carried away.

3.The Curse of Mr. Bean

Mr Bean goes to a swimming pool but finds himself terrified of the high diving board, afterwards, he must try several imaginative ways to get out of the car park when he doesn't have enough money to pay.

4.Mr. Bean Goes To Town

Mr Bean buys a portable television but can't get it to work properly.

5.The Trouble with Mr. Bean

Mr Bean rushes to the dentist's office after oversleeping, then helps a boy fix his remote-controlled toy boat, and later is menaced by a wasp while having a picnic.

6.Mr. Bean Rides Again

Mr Bean resuscitates a heart attack victim at the bus stop, has problems mailing a letter after accidentally swallowing the stamp, and decides to go on holiday.

7.Merry Christmas Mr. Bean

Mr Bean goes Christmas shopping, conducts a Salvation Army band, and has Christmas dinner with his long-suffering girlfriend.

8.Mr. Bean in Room 426

Mr Bean treats himself to a weekend at a posh hotel where he manages to get himself into one embarrassing situation after another.

9.Do-It-Yourself Mr. Bean

Mr Bean hosts a New Year's Eve party, discovers some bargains at a sale, and causes havoc when he decorates his room.

10.Mind the Baby, Mr. Bean

Mr Bean goes to the funfair and is surprised to discover that he has become a babysitter. Luckily, the mother finds them in time.

11.Back to School Mr. Bean

Mr Bean goes back to school where he takes part in a science demonstration, attends an art class and learns the finer points of judo.

12.Tee Off, Mr. Bean

Mr Bean goes to the laundrette where he loses his trousers once more; Mr Bean plays a round of minigolf but has difficulty keeping his ball on the green.

13.Goodnight Mr. Bean

Mr Bean has a hard time waiting his turn at the hospital, later he visits Windsor Castle and, that night, has trouble with a bit of insomnia.

14.Hair by Mr. Bean of London

Mr Bean has a grand day at the hairdresser's, enters Teddy in a pet show and has to find his way off a train when he loses his ticket.

15.The Best Bits of Mr. Bean

During a rainy day, Bean ventures into his loft to find an umbrella. As he shifts through his stored items, he begins to reminisce his past adventures, including his dental appointment, the church service, and the time his Mini was crushed by an Army Tank.
