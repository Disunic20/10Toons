The Curse of Mr. Bean

Season 1,Episode 3

About

Mr Bean goes to a swimming pool but finds himself terrified of the high diving board, afterwards, he must try several imaginative ways to get out of the car park when he doesn't have enough money to pay. He then demonstrates the proper way to make a sandwich whilst out at the park. Finally, when Mr Bean and his girlfriend go on a date to watch a horror movie he teases her for being scared until the film actually starts.
